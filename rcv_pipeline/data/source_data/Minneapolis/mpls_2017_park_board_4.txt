2017 Park Board District 4 results
Official Winner: Jono Cowgill
November 7, 2017

Municipal Election

This was a ranked-choice voting (RCV) race. Voters were asked to rank up to 3 different candidates. After one round of tabulation, Jono Cowgill was elected.
