Ranked-choice Voting Tabulation Center Summary Statement

2017 Park Board District 5 results
Official Winner: Steffanie Musich
November 7, 2017

Municipal Election

This was a ranked-choice voting (RCV) race. Voters were asked to rank up to 3 different candidates. After one round of tabulation, Steffanie Musich was elected.
