2021 City Council Ward 4 election results
Official winner: LaTrisha Vetaw
November 2, 2021

Municipal Election

This was a ranked-choice voting (RCV) race. Voters were asked to rank up to 3 different candidates.

Candidate LaTrisha Vetaw received enough first-choice votes to be elected and is the winner.
